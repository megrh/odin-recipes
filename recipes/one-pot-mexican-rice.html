One Pot Mexican Rice (Vegan!)

A quick, easy vegan dish which leaves you with only one pot to wash up!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 white or red onion
  • 1 cup of white rice washed and rinsed
  • 1 tin black beans cooked and drained
  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin sweetcorn
  • 1 avocado
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne (to taste)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • Handful of fresh coriander

STEPS

  1. Heat oil in a large, lidded pan
  2. Gently fry onions, garlic and peppers
  3. Add paprika, cumin, salt, pepper and cayenne
  4. Add rice and fry until the rice is shining and coated in seasoning, stirring constantly
  5. Pour in tin of tomatoes and stir, bringing to a simmer
  6. Add in black beans
  7. Place lid on the pot and continue to simmer for 15 minutes. Do not remove the lid before 15 minutes is up. Then, remove lid and stir to check consistency. It may need 5-10 more minutes.
  8. Plate up your rice and serve with chopped avocado and coriander.
