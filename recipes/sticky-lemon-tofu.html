Sticky Lemon Tofu

Duration: 30 mins

Bite sized pieces of tofu in a sticky, tangy and sweet lemon sauce. You won't miss your old Chinese takeaway with this recipe!

INGREDIENTS

For the crispy tofu

  • 400g extra firm tofu, drained
  • 30g cornflour
  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp (each) salt and pepper

For the sticky lemon sauce

  • 60ml lemon juice (roughly 2 lemons)
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 60ml water
  • 1/4tsp stock powder
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 50g sugar
  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp cornstarch, mixed with 2tsp water to make a slurry

Steps

  1. Drain the tofu and cut it into bite size cubes
  2. Drizzle soy sauce over cubes and stir gently
  3. Add cornstarch, season with salt and pepper and stir until all tofu is coated
  4. Heat ol in a large frying pan. Once hot, add tfu cubes an fry until golden brown. Turn using tongs or a spatula to get them evenly browned and cooked.
  5. Once cooked, transfer to a plate so you can use the pan to make the sauce
  6. Add remaining oil, heat, and add minced ginger and garlic to fry for 1 minute.
  7. Next, add water, stock powder, soy sauce, lemon juice/zest, sugar and stir together.
  8. Finally, add the cornstarch slurry and simmer the sauce until it's stick and sticky. Stir frequently.
  9. Combine tofu with the sauce and serve with rice or noodles and your choice of stir fried veggies. Garnish with spring onions or sesame seeds.
  10. Enjoy!
